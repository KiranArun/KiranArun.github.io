I work with Acorn Aspirations

I’ve created an AI learning platform to empower young people with technical AI skills and knowledge. My workshops were part of the AI bootcamp for TeensInAI.

I have written Jupyter Notebooks which:

  • Use modern tooling (Python, Tensorflow, Jupyter)
  • Teach the fundamentals (Linear Algebra, Calculus, Neural Nets, etc)
  • Set practical challenges (Create a Neural Net to classify images)

My Notebooks are designed for Google’s Colaboratory (a powerful, free, AI research environment), find them on GitHub.
