Why I Do This

Why I left school (aged 15, UK Year 10)

  • My time and mental energy were being used unproductively
  • The GCSE system forced me to work towards achieving a high-average
  • I aspire to achieve great things but structured mass schooling will erode my competitiveness and demotivate me
  • I don’t mind failing, because it gains you a visible, quantifiable error from which to learn

Why I learn ML

  • It’s the future and it’s cutting-edge
  • I enjoy the construction of a project/system (of models, maths and computational systems)
  • Potential to make a big positive contribution to the nation of Earth.

Why I Publish on Github

  • Sharing allows my work to help others and others to help me
  • I need somewhere to document my history, allowing me to move on
  • It’s my Résumé

Why I use iPython/Jupyter notebooks instead of pen & paper (or Word™)

  • I learn to command modern, leading-edge compute tools
  • It combines maths, programming and easy documentation so it uses my time efficiently
  • I get a good Résumé while I’m still young

Note: My Junior and Senior schools were amongst the best in my country and I remember them fondly, also I must credit them with my development
